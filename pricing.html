Common questions
Why are annual plans only on the website?
Apple's App Store limits in-app subscription prices to $999.99/year. Our higher-tier annual plans exceed that cap, so we offer them directly here — with no middleman and more savings passed to you.
How do I access my plan after purchasing?
After completing checkout, you'll receive a confirmation email. Your Ecofitt account will be upgraded automatically within a few minutes. If it doesn't activate, email us at [email protected].
Can I cancel or switch plans?
Annual plans are billed once per year. You can cancel renewal at any time before your next billing date. To switch plans mid-year, contact support and we'll prorate the difference.
Is payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. Ecofitt never stores your card details.
What's the difference between annual plans and the monthly plans in the app?
They unlock the same features — annual plans simply save you 25% and are billed once a year. Monthly plans are available in the Ecofitt app through Apple's standard in-app purchase system.
